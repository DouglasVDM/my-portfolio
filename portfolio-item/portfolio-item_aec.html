Advancing Engaged Citizenship

A platform where students can journey with a team of mentors to assist with the innovation of project ideas, during the conceptualization and implementation phase. It allows students to complete a standard template with prompts to answer key questions. It also allows university staff to provide feedback and mentorship via the platform.

  1. Students can create social impact projects that addresses a need in communities and request support from mentors.
  2. Mentors can view all projects created by students. Additionally, they provide support to students by means of a messaging system.
  3. Admin can add additional admin , mentors and post competitions.
